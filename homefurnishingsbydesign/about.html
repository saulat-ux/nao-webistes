About Us

Creating beautiful, functional spaces that inspire

Meet Sarah Johnson

With over 15 years of experience in interior design, Sarah Johnson founded Inspired Interiors with a passion for creating spaces that truly reflect her clients' personalities and lifestyles. Her approach combines timeless design principles with contemporary trends, resulting in interiors that are both beautiful and functional.

Sarah holds a Bachelor's degree in Interior Design from the Rhode Island School of Design and has worked on projects ranging from intimate residential spaces to large commercial developments. Her work has been featured in several design publications, and she's known for her ability to transform any space into something extraordinary.

Our Mission

At Inspired Interiors, we believe that great design has the power to transform not just spaces, but lives. Our mission is to create interiors that are not only visually stunning but also deeply personal and functional. We work closely with each client to understand their unique needs, preferences, and lifestyle, ensuring that every design decision reflects their individual story.

We are committed to sustainable design practices, sourcing eco-friendly materials whenever possible, and creating timeless designs that won't need frequent updates. Our goal is to craft spaces that our clients will love for years to come.
