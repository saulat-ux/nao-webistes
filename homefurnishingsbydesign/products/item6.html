Handwoven Area Rug

This beautiful handwoven area rug combines natural jute and soft cotton fibers to create a piece that's both durable and comfortable underfoot. The neutral geometric pattern adds visual interest while remaining versatile enough to complement any decor style.

Features:

  • Handwoven construction for durability
  • Natural jute and cotton fiber blend
  • Geometric pattern in neutral tones
  • Available size: 8' x 10' (96" x 120")
  • Low pile height for easy maintenance
  • Suitable for high-traffic areas

Styling Tips:

This versatile rug works beautifully in living rooms, dining rooms, or bedrooms. The natural fibers and neutral colors make it easy to layer with other textiles and decor. Use it to define seating areas, anchor furniture groupings, or add warmth to hardwood or tile floors. The geometric pattern adds subtle visual interest without overwhelming the space.
