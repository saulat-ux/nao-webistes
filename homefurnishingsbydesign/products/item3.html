Decorative Wall Mirror

This striking sunburst mirror features intricate metal detailing that creates dramatic shadows and visual interest on your walls. The reflective surface helps bounce light around the room while the sculptural frame serves as stunning wall art.

Features:

  • Sunburst design with metal frame construction
  • High-quality mirror glass with beveled edge
  • Antique brass finish with patina details
  • Dimensions: 36" diameter overall, 12" mirror diameter
  • Ready to hang with included hardware
  • Suitable for indoor use in dry environments

Styling Tips:

This dramatic mirror works beautifully as a focal point above a console table, fireplace mantel, or in an entryway. The sunburst design adds texture and movement to walls, while the reflective surface helps make rooms appear larger and brighter. Consider grouping with smaller mirrors for a gallery wall effect.
